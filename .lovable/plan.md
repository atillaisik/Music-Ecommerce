
# SoundScape — Music Instrument E-commerce Website

A visually rich, dark-themed music instrument showcase website matching the Figma prototype. All data will be static/mock (no real purchasing). 

## Design System
- **Dark theme** with deep blacks/dark grays as the primary background
- **Red accent color** for CTAs and highlights ("STARTS HERE.", "Explore Instruments" button)
- **Bold, uppercase typography** for headings
- Stats bar with large numbers (50K+ Products, 200+ Brands, 1M+ Musicians)

## Pages

### 1. Homepage
- **Hero section**: Full-width background image of a guitarist, "YOUR SOUND STARTS HERE." headline, subtitle text, two CTA buttons (Explore Instruments, Shop Best Sellers), "NEW ARRIVALS 2024" badge
- **Stats bar**: 50K+ Products, 200+ Brands, 1M+ Musicians
- **Browse Categories**: Instrument category cards (Guitars, Drums, Keyboards, etc.) with images
- **Featured/Best Sellers section**: Product cards with images, names, prices, and ratings
- **Promotional banner** or brand showcase section

### 2. Shop Page
- Product grid with filter sidebar (by category, brand, price range)
- Product cards showing image, name, price, rating, and "Add to Cart" button
- Sort options (price, popularity, newest)

### 3. Instruments Page
- Categorized instrument browsing (Guitars, Bass, Drums, Keyboards, DJ Equipment, etc.)
- Category cards leading to filtered product views

### 4. Brands Page
- Brand logo grid/list showcasing partner brands (Fender, Gibson, Yamaha, Roland, etc.)
- Click-through to filtered products by brand

### 5. Learn Page
- Educational content cards (guides, tutorials, tips for beginners)
- Articles or resource links for musicians

### 6. Deals Page
- Special offer products with original/sale prices
- Promotional banners and limited-time deals

### 7. Contact Page
- Contact form (name, email, message)
- Store location info and support details

## Shared Components
- **Navigation bar**: Logo "SOUNDSCAPE", main nav links (Shop, Instruments, Brands, Learn, Deals, Contact), search icon, wishlist heart, cart with badge count, user icon, "SHOP INSTRUMENTS" CTA button
- **Footer**: Site links, social media, newsletter signup
- **Product Card**: Reusable component with image, title, price, rating, and action button

## Interactions
- Smooth page navigation via React Router
- Hover effects on product cards and buttons
- Mobile-responsive layout with hamburger menu
